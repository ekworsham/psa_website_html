Landscape Maintenance

ProScapes of Atlanta delivers proactive, detail-driven landscape maintenance designed to protect and enhance the long-term value of each property we serve. Our approach emphasizes consistency, communication, and accountability—ensuring every visit is performed to a clearly defined standard. Clients can expect well-trained crews, professional equipment, and a comprehensive scope of services that includes turf care, pruning, seasonal enhancements, and site cleanliness. Each property is managed with a tailored plan that reflects its unique needs, usage, and aesthetic goals.


Beyond routine service, ProScapes of Atlanta is committed to being a responsive, solutions-oriented partner. We conduct regular site evaluations, provide transparent reporting, and proactively identify opportunities to improve safety, appearance, and plant health. Our team works closely with property managers, HOAs, and ownership groups to ensure expectations are met, issues are addressed promptly, and the landscape consistently represents the quality and professionalism of the community or commercial environment.
