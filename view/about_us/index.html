OWNERS

John Estes is the Founder of ProScapes of Atlanta and a respected industry leader with more than 30 years of experience across all sectors of the landscaping industry, including residential, commercial, HOA, and mixed-use properties. His deep, hands-on knowledge spans landscape maintenance, design-build, irrigation, enhancements, and long-term asset protection, providing clients with comprehensive, practical solutions.

John founded ProScapes of Atlanta in 2004 with a commitment to quality, accountability, and people-first leadership. Under his guidance, the company has grown into a trusted partner for property managers and communities throughout the region, known for delivering consistent results, fostering strong teams, and building long-term relationships through reliable, high-performance landscape services..

Keith Worsham joined ProScapes of Atlanta in 2014 as an equal partner, bringing with him over 30years of experience and a well-rounded background in the landscape industry. His career began in 1988, and his progression from entry-level roles to partner and owner has provided him with a deep, practical understanding of all facets of landscape operations and management.

Keith's leadership, industry knowledge, and strong professional reputation have played a key role in strengthening ProScapes of Atlanta's HOA and residential service divisions, while also helping guide the company's successful expansion into the commercial landscape sector. His commitment to quality, client relationships, and team development continues to support the company's growth and long-term success.

WHERE WE CAME FROM

Originally established as a residential landscape maintenance company specializing in plant and lawn care, ProScapes of Atlanta expanded into landscape installation services in 2005 and was formally incorporated under its current name. Since then, the company has evolved into an award-winning organization, recognized for exceptional craftsmanship, consistent performance, and an unwavering commitment to customer satisfaction.

MISSION STATEMENT

At ProScapes of Atlanta, our mission is to enhance, protect, and elevate the value of HOA, commercial, and mixed-use communities through consistent, professional landscape management. We are committed to treating every property as our own by delivering dependable service, proactive communication, and disciplined execution on every visit.

CORE VALUES

We believe successful communities are built on accountability, proactive care, and long-term stewardship. By partnering with property managers and boards, we ensure landscapes are not only well maintained, but thoughtfully managed to reflect pride, safety, and excellence year-round.
